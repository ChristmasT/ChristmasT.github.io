LJ1 Presentation

Hello everyone, This is Mike.

Today, our main focus is this picture.

I see

A pretty horrible picture, isn't it? Then what do you see on the picture?

  1. I see the whole picture is set in the tone of dark red
  2. I see a river in the middle of the image.
  3. I see lots of shabby houses built along the river.
  4. I see a woman crouching near the river with a bowl.
  5. etc

I think

Right now, I believe you already have lots of profound thoughts about this photo. Here are some of mine.

  1. I think the dark red color precisely conveys how poor and terrible the environment is.
  2. I think the river might be the only water source for the citizens here.
  3. I think people there are still live in a fairly primitive way due to lack of development and poor infrastructures.
  4. I think the woman was washing her clothes in the muddy river or to make the matter worse, she was probably washing the food prepared for the whole family.
  5. etc

I wonder

Having looked at the photo, there is indeed something that we should consider about.

  1. I wonder how long will they live under such unbearable circumstances.
  2. I wonder how Singapore strived to be a powerful country from this situation.
  3. I wonder how many people today are still suffering from similar perishing sanitation systems.

So just look around, to appreciate how wonderful your living conditions are compared to theirs. You have clean water to wash your face and clothes; You have favorite phones and laptops; You can sit in such comfortable class and meanwhile have the leisure to listen to my speech. How lucky we are! So please, widely open your eyes, you might discover that the people and things beside you are actually the most precious fortune that you've ever had.

Thank you!
