8.13 Paragraphs writing

2018/8/15 posted in  Monday

P15 Activity 11:

Topic: a job that interests you.

Paragraph:

Film editor is a person who can proficiently cut a film or short movie with professional applications and make profit from it. Nowadays, it is significantly important for unprofessional individuals to condense their special memory into a short trailer. Therefore there is no surprise to find out that film editors can gain great amount of money within short amount of time. However, the images for film editors are usually some overweighted persons with red and sleepy eyes. As a matter of fact, that is not always the case. Since these people are willing to devote their time for editing film, they must find editing interesting and worthwhile. Furthermore, they can also maintain a good shape through working out regularly in the interval of two orders. Hence being a film editor may be one of my favorite occupations in the future.

P27 Activity 10:

Comparison targets:

Taking note with electronic devices and traditionally taking note with pen and book.

Paragraph:

Today is 2018 when everything is trending in becoming digital. Thus, it is extremely difficult for college students to choose if they should take notes with their tablets and laptops. Note-taking on electronic devices has way more convenience in transferring data through cloud service. Also it has a prominent advantage that traditional books don't have which is the weight students take to class are significantly lighter. Nevertheless, that doesn't mean the traditional way is an outdated way. Countless researches internationally have shown that handwriting has more assistance in helping students to memorize knowledge. What's more is that with a mere set of pen and notebook, listeners are less likely to be distracted by the messages popping up. Therefore in conclusion, these two ways of taking note both have their pros and cons, what undergraduates should do is wisely choose the tools in different circumstances. After all, the method is not the thing that matters, the knowledge is.
