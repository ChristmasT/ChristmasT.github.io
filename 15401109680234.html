Family Values Of NUS Undergraduates

Hypothesis: Singapore undergraduates consider family as an important component of their happiness. They are dependent on their families.
Gender: ⬜ Male ⬜ Female

Nationality: ⬜ Singapore ⬜ Malaysia ⬜ China ⬜ India ⬜ others
Faculty:

  1. How much time do you spend with(online or face-to-face) family in one week?
    A. more than seven hours
    B. more than three hours, less than seven hours
    C. more than an hour, less than three hours
    D. less than an hour

  2. How many hours do you think is proper to spend with family members each day?
    A. more than 1 hour
    B. more than 0.5 hour and less than 1 hour
    C. less than 0.5 hour

  3. Do you think getting together with family members every day is a good and relaxing thing?
    A. yes
    B. no

  4. What do you do when getting together with your family members? ____________________________________________________

  5. Do you reach out for your family for help when you are? (Multiple Choices)
    ⬜ experiencing stress and difficulties
    ⬜ having financial problems
    ⬜ feeling bored
    ⬜ in hospital

  6. You chose your major
    A.independently
    B. with your family’s advice
    C. both
    7.(1) How does your family contribute to your sense of well being?
    A. my family does not contribute to my well being
    B. it is important to my well being mainly because it is my source of emotional support
    C. it is important to my well being mainly because it is my source of financial support
    (2) If yes, why do you think so?

  7. Does your family expect you to give an allowance to them when you start working?
    A. yes
    B. no

  8. Are you willing to give an allowance to your parents when you start working?
    A. yes
    B. no
